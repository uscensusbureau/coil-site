Past Programs

  • Census Accelerate

    Paired professional creatives with community organizations to develop search-optimized digital content to increase 2020 Census response rates.

  • Census Solutions Workshops

    Taught Census Bureau employees new and creative strategies to achieve their mission while focusing on the needs of communities they are serving.

  • Open Data for Good Grand Challenge

    Advanced some of the most promising technology solutions built with open data and rewarded exemplary uses of federal open data.

  • Get Out the Count Video Challenge

    Rewarded the most impactful, accurate, inspiring, funny, creative, and culturally relevant videos to educate the masses about the 2020 Census and mobilized communities to respond.
